Piccolo F2805xM

Key features

  • 60-MHz, 32-bit Fixed-Point C28x™ CPU
  • 64-kB or 128-kB embedded Flash, up to 20kB RAM
  • 12-bit ADC, up to 3.74 MSPS
  • 150-ps high-resolution ePWM
  • Motor ROM including FAST™ software encoder
    to enable InstaSPIN™-FOC  solutions
  • Motor ROM including SpinTAC™ Suite to enable
    InstaSPIN™-MOTION solutions


Key benefits

  • Unique, precision control peripherals
  • Lowers system cost
  • Increased ease of use
  • Small package size
